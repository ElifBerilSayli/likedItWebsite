To get analyze of your watching, first, you should sign up. At the homepage, you can sign up with your Google account to LikedIt. After signing, on the homepage, you can see the last six videos you like on YouTube. You can evaluate your decision with LikedIt.


After signing up to LikedIt, please make calibration to get a healthy analysis. You can do this from the calibration page in the sidebar.


The calibration page is for making your analysis healthier. In this page, you should click each blue circle, while you are looking at. After your click, it should be turned into black. After you make all circles black you can go back to the homepage by "Done" button. If you don't do the calibration, then your heatmap can have some problems. If you feel like heatmap doesn't show correct results, then you can repeat the calibration. Then you ready to watch videos.


We respect our client's privacy, so we delete any personal data (different from feedback) immediately after analyzing it. If you still don't want to share your images with us, you can choose to use our desktop application. You can found it from 'Settings'. If you choose 'Local' option, you can install the application easily by download the installer and follow the installation guideline. In this way, you can keep your images on your personal computer. If you don't save any preference for this setting, it is accepted as a cloud.


For watching a video you can click a video at the homepage or search for a specific video by using the search bar at the bottom of the homepage. For searching it is enough to write a word with English characters to the bar and then press 'Enter' or the click the search icon. After that 12 videos will be shown on the page. To watch one of them, again, you can just click.


After clicking a video, you are directed to the player page. On this page, in the beginning, the system wants permission to access the camera of the computer. If you give the permission - if you want to get an analysis, you should - then there will be a little box that shows your face via the camera. This box is for you to adjust the position for proper gaze data. You should arrange yourself as fitting the green face to your own face. After fitting you can start the video and the box including your face will disappear. If you try to close the page before the process of transmission of data is completed, the page will warn you that the analysis can be affected.


You can see your previous analyses on 'Analyses' page. You can press any one of the videos to see your specific analysis. If you had never watched a video, this page will be empty and there will be a text that says 'Please watch a video to get feedback'. If you didn't sign in, there will be a text that says 'Please sign in'.


When you click on an analysis on the analyses page, you are redirected to the feedback page of that specific analysis. There, you can see your heatmap of gaze. The heatmap can be watched by playing the video. Also, there are two plots of your liking scores and emotions scores. You can choose the type of analysis that you want to see by clicking like analysis or emotion analysis button. You can click on a point at the plot to seek that second at the video and watch it from there. There are also buttons of pause/resume to help you to navigate. When stopped, the heatmap of the gaze will also be stopped.


In 'LikedIt Trends' page, you can see our trend videos in LikedIt. At the top, there are 6 videos that most liked according to our analysis. Then, you can see the happiest 6 videos and 6 most surprising videos. On this page, you can select and watch a video by clicking it. Below the videos, you can see the rates and number of views.
